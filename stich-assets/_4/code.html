Clarifying the Pulse.

Frequently asked questions about our logistics and performance standards.

What factors influence the performance pricing?

expand_more

Pricing is calculated based on four core technical pillars:

Scale

Number of lions and musicians required.

Complexity

Difficulty of stunts (Jong/High-Pole) and duration.

Geography

Travel logistics and site accessibility.

Customization

LED integration, specific colors, or logo props.

What is the recommended booking timeframe?

expand_more

For major corporate events and weddings, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance. For Lunar New Year festivities, availability often reaches capacity 8-10 months prior. Last-minute inquiries are subject to artist availability.

How can we customize the performance?

expand_more

We bridge tradition with modern branding through several avenues:

  • • LED Lion Costumes for nighttime or club environments.
  • • Custom-printed banners or "Cheng" items featuring your corporate logo.
  • • Music sets blended with contemporary tracks for a hybrid experience.
